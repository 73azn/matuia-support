Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +
You can reset your password by going to the Settings page in the app and clicking "Forgot Password".
Is my data safe? +
Yes, we prioritize your privacy. All report data is encrypted locally and we do not share your information with third parties.
How do I delete my account? +
To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us using the form above or check the "Privacy" section in the app settings.
